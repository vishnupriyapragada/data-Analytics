What you should know about Heart Disease

Heart disease, also known as cardiovascular disease, encompasses a range of conditions that affect the heart. Here are some key points you should be aware of:

  • Common Types: Heart disease includes conditions like coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and more.
  • Risk Factors: Major risk factors for heart disease include high blood pressure, high cholesterol, smoking, obesity, diabetes, and a family history of heart disease.
  • Warning Signs: Symptoms of heart disease can vary, but they may include chest pain, shortness of breath, fatigue, and irregular heartbeats.
  • Prevention: Lifestyle changes such as a heart-healthy diet, regular exercise, and not smoking can significantly reduce the risk of heart disease.
  • Treatment: Heart disease can be managed through medication, lifestyle changes, and, in some cases, surgical interventions like angioplasty or bypass surgery.
